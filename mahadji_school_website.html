About Us

Shaping Tomorrow's Leaders

Founded in 1985, Mahadji Mohamed El Habib High School stands as a beacon of academic excellence in Bir el Djir, Oran. Our institution blends a rigorous curriculum with strong ethical foundations, nurturing students to become confident, capable, and compassionate citizens.

With over 1,800 students and 120 dedicated educators, we are committed to providing a world-class secondary education that prepares each learner for higher education and life beyond the classroom.
